# ISRO ke Rockets: Launch ke Peeche ki Science, Aasaan Bhasha Mein

Har baar jab Sriharikota se ISRO ka koi rocket launch hota hai, toh hum sabka seena garv se chauda ho jaata hai. Woh countdown ki awaaz... "Ten, nine, eight...", phir engine se nikalti aag aur dhuyein ka gubaar, aur dekhte hi dekhte ek massive rocket aasmaan ko cheerte hue upar chala jaata hai. Chandrayaan ho ya Mangalyaan, yeh manzar hamesha goosebumps de jaata hai!

Lekin kya aapne kabhi socha hai ki yeh jaadu hota kaise hai? Itna bhaari, several hundred tonnes ka rocket, gravity ko maat dekar space mein kaise pahunch jaata hai? Rocket science ko log aksar 'bohot difficult' maante hain, par tension not! Aaj hum iske peeche ki science ko bilkul simple bhasha mein, "aam aadmi" style mein samjhenge.

Toh chaliye, apni virtual seatbelt baandh lijiye, hum ISRO ke launchpad se science ki duniya mein udne waale hain!

## Rocket Science ka Sabse Bada Rule: Newton's Third Law

School mein hum sabne Newton's Third Law of Motion padha tha - "For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction." Yaad hai? Shayad thoda-thoda. Yehi ek line poori rocket science ka foundation hai.

**Ise ek simple analogy se samajhte hain:**
Imagine kijiye aapne ek balloon (gubbaara) mein hawa bhari aur use bina baandhe chhod diya. Kya hoga? Hawa tezi se neeche (ya peeche) niklegi (ye hai **Action**), aur balloon utni hi tezi se upar (ya aage) bhaagega (ye hai **Reaction**).

Rocket bilkul isi principle par kaam karta hai.
-   **Action:** Rocket ke engine se beintehaa garmi aur pressure ke saath high-velocity exhaust gases (dhuan) neeche ki taraf nikalti hain.
-   **Reaction:** Ye gases zameen ko dhakka nahi de rahi, balki woh khud rocket ko upar ki taraf ek zordaar dhakka (jise **Thrust** kehte hain) deti hain.

Thrust itna powerful hona chahiye ki woh rocket ke vajan (weight) aur Earth ki gravity, dono se zyaada ho. Jab thrust jeet jaata hai, tabhi rocket 'liftoff' karta hai. Simple, hai na?

## Rocket ka 'Fuel Tank': Propellants aur Engines

Ab sawaal yeh hai ki itna powerful thrust paida karne ke liye 'action' kahan se aata hai? Jawaab hai: **Propellants**. Ye rocket ka fuel hota hai. Propellant mein do cheezein hoti hain:
1.  **Fuel:** Jo jalta hai.
2.  **Oxidizer:** Jo jalne mein madad karta hai (kyunki space mein oxygen toh hai nahi).

ISRO ke rockets, jaise PSLV aur LVM3 (GSLV Mk3), mainly do tarah ke propellants use karte hain.

### 1. Solid Propellants (Thos Indhan)
Yeh bilkul ek Diwali rocket jaisa hai. Ismein fuel aur oxidizer pehle se hi mix karke ek solid cake ki tarah bhare hote hain.
-   **Kaise kaam karta hai:** Ek baar ismein aag laga di, toh yeh tab tak jalta rahega jab tak poora fuel khatm na ho jaaye. Aap ise beech mein rok nahi sakte ya iski speed kam-zyada nahi kar sakte.
-   **Fayda:** Yeh bohot zyaada aur instant thrust deta hai. Isliye ISRO ise rockets ke 'strap-on boosters' (side mein lage mote-mote boosters) mein use karta hai, taaki shuruaat mein rocket ko zameen se uthane ke liye zordaar dhakka mil sake.
-   **Nuksaan:** Koi control nahi hota. Ek baar chala diya, toh chala diya.

### 2. Liquid Propellants (Taral Indhan)
Yeh aapki car ya bike ke engine jaisa hai. Ismein fuel aur oxidizer alag-alag tanks mein liquid form mein rakhe jaate hain.
-   **Kaise kaam karta hai:** Launch ke time in dono liquids ko pipes ke zariye ek 'combustion chamber' mein bheja jaata hai, jahan woh milte hain aur jalte hain.
-   **Fayda:** Iska sabse bada fayda hai **control**. Engineers iske flow ko control kar sakte hain, engine ko band karke dobara chala sakte hain, aur thrust ko kam ya zyada kar sakte hain. Yehi control satellite ko ek aakhri, achanak aur bahut zaroori dhakke dekar usko apne sahi orbit mein place karne ke liye zaroori hai. ISRO ka famous **Vikas Engine** ek liquid propellant engine hi hai.

## Cryogenic Engines: ISRO ka 'Baahubali'

Aapne GSLV rocket ke launch ke time 'Cryogenic Upper Stage' ka naam zaroor suna hoga. Yeh ISRO ki sabse badi achievements mein se ek hai. Cryogenic ka matlab hota hai 'bohot hi thanda'.

-   **Kya hai yeh:** Is engine mein Liquid Hydrogen (-253°C par) ko fuel ki tarah aur Liquid Oxygen (-183°C par) ko oxidizer ki tarah use kiya jaata hai. In gases ko itne thande temperature par liquid banakar store karna hi apne aap mein ek bohot bada engineering challenge hai.
-   **Kyun hai yeh 'Baahubali'?:** Cryogenic propellants bohot hi zyada efficient hote hain. Ye "per kilogram of fuel" sabse zyada thrust dete hain. Iska matlab hai ki aap kam fuel mein zyaada power generate kar sakte hain. Heavy satellites (jaise communication satellites) ko Geostationary Orbit (zameen se 36,000 km upar) tak le jaane ke liye is high-efficiency engine ki zaroorat padti hai.

Yeh technology itni complex hai ki duniya ke kuch hi deshon ke paas hai, aur Bharat unmein se ek hai!

## Gravity ko 'Dhoka' Dena: Staging ka Kamaal

Aapne dekha hoga ki rocket upar jaate-jaate apne hisse (parts) neeche girata jaata hai. Ise **Staging** kehte hain.

**Iski analogy sochiye:** Aap ek trek par jaa rahe hain. Aapne ek bada, bhaari bag uthaya hai. Pehle base camp tak pahunchne ke baad, aap us bag ka kuch samaan wahin chhod dete hain taaki aage ki chadhai aasaan ho jaaye. Phir doosre camp par thoda aur samaan chhod dete hain. Aisa karne se aap lighter hote jaate hain aur kam energy mein upar tak pahunch paate hain.

Rocket bhi yehi karta hai.
-   **Stage 1:** Shuruaat mein solid boosters rocket ko uthate hain. Fuel khatm hote hi, unhe rocket se alag kar diya jaata hai (samudra mein gira diya jaata hai). Ab rocket kaafi halka ho gaya.
-   **Stage 2:** Phir agla stage, jo usually liquid engine hota hai, start hota hai aur rocket ko aur upar le jaata hai. Iska fuel khatm hone par yeh bhi alag ho jaata hai.
-   **Upper Stage:** Aakhir mein, chhota sa upper stage (jaise Cryogenic stage) bachta hai, jiska kaam hota hai satellite ko uski final orbit mein aakhri dhakka dekar azaad karna.

Is tarah 'dead weight' (khaali fuel tanks) ko hatate jaane se rocket bohot efficient ho jaata hai aur aasaani se space tak pahunch paata hai.

## Seedha Upar Kyun Nahi Jaate? The Gravity Turn

Ek aakhri interesting baat. Aapne notice kiya hoga ki rocket launch ke thodi der baad seedha upar jaane ke bajaaye, thoda tirchha (curve) hone lagta hai. Aisa kyun?

Rocket ka maksad sirf upar jaana nahi hai. Uska asli kaam hai satellite ko **orbital velocity** dena.
-   **Orbital Velocity:** Yeh woh minimum horizontal (sideways) speed hai jo ek satellite ko chahiye taaki woh Earth ki gravity mein girne ke bajaaye, uske chaaron taraf chakkar lagata rahe. Yeh aisi sthiti hai ki satellite lagaatar zameen ki taraf gir to raha hota hai lekin itni aage ki speed ke kaaran woh kabhi zameen se takraata hi nahi.

Agar rocket seedha upar jaata rahega, toh woh aakhir mein waapis neeche aa jayega (jaise aap ball ko upar fekte hain). Isliye, rocket ko upar bhi jaana hai aur sideways speed bhi pakadni hai. Launch ke thodi der baad rocket dheere-dheere tirchha hona shuru hota hai. Is technique ko **Gravity Turn** kehte hain. Yeh sabse zyada fuel-efficient tareeka hai altitude (unchai) aur velocity (gati) dono haasil karne ka.

Toh agli baar jab aap ISRO ka launch dekhein, toh aap sirf aag aur dhuan nahi, balki uske peeche kaam kar rahe Newton's Law, alag-alag tarah ke engines, staging ka kamaal, aur gravity turn ki brilliant physics ko bhi appreciate kar payenge.

## Mukhya Baatein (Key Takeaways)

Agar aap poora article bhool bhi jaayein, toh bas yeh 5 baatein yaad rakhiyega:

-   **Newton's Third Law:** Rocket ka basic principle. Garam gas neeche (Action), rocket upar (Reaction). Bilkul ek balloon ki tarah.
-   **Propellants:** Rocket ka fuel. **Solid Propellants** (Diwali rocket jaisa) shuruaati dhakke ke liye aur **Liquid Propellants** (bike engine jaisa) control aur precision ke liye.
-   **Cryogenic Engine:** ISRO ka 'Baahubali' engine. Super-cooled liquid hydrogen aur oxygen use karta hai, jo bohot powerful aur efficient hota hai. Bhaari satellites ke liye zaroori hai.
-   **Multi-staging:** Rocket upar jaate hue apne khaali fuel tanks (stages) ko girata jaata hai taaki vajan kam ho aur speed badhe.
-   **Gravity Turn:** Rocket seedha upar nahi jaata, balki ek curve path leta hai. Aisa isliye taaki woh unchai ke saath-saath satellite ko orbit mein ghumne ke liye zaroori horizontal speed bhi de sake.
