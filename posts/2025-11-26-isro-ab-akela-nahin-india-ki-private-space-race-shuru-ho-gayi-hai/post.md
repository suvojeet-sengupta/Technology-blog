# ISRO Ab Akela Nahin: India ki Private Space Race Shuru Ho Gayi Hai

Hum sab ISRO ke fan hain. Bachpan se humne Chandrayaan ki landing dekhi hai, Mangalyaan ke successful mission par garv kiya hai, aur ISRO ke har launch ke liye countdown mein shaamil hue hain. ISRO humare liye sirf ek space agency nahin, ek emotion hai. Par ab is kahani mein ek naya, exciting twist aa gaya hai. ISRO ab is race mein akela nahin hai!

Ek naye daur ki shuruaat ho chuki hai jahan Skyroot, Agnikul, Pixxel jaise naye naam ubhar kar aa rahe hain. Yeh private companies ab India ke space ambitions ko naye पंख (wings) de rahi hain. Ye India ki apni "private space race" hai, aur yeh scene bilkul badalne wali hai.

Toh chaliye, dive karte hain is naye space revolution mein aur samajhte hain ki iska matlab kya hai, kaun hain yeh naye khiladi, aur isse India ko kya fayda hone wala hai.

## Pehle vs. Ab: Kya Badla Hai?

Ab tak, India mein space se juda har kaam—rocket banane se lekar satellite launch karne tak—sirf aur sirf ISRO karti thi. Yeh ek government monopoly thi. Soch ke dekhiye, jaise 90s mein sirf BSNL/MTNL ka landline hota tha. Agar aapko satellite launch karna hai, toh aapke paas sirf ek hi option tha: ISRO.

Lekin 2020 mein, Indian government ne space sector ko private players ke liye khol diya. Yeh ek game-changing decision tha. Iske liye ek special body banayi gayi jiska naam hai **IN-SPACe (Indian National Space Promotion and Authorization Center)**.

Simple shabdon mein, IN-SPACe private companies ke liye ek 'single-window' system hai. Yeh unka dost, philosopher aur guide hai. Agar kisi private company ko:
- Rocket launch karna hai.
- ISRO ki facilities (jaise launchpads ya testing labs) use karni hai.
- Technology transfer chahiye.

...toh unhein 10 alag-alag departments ke chakkar nahi kaatne padenge. Woh seedha IN-SPACe ke paas jayenge, aur IN-SPACe unki madad karega. Is ek kadam ne India mein private space startups ki बाढ़ (flood) la di hai.

## Kaun Hain Yeh Naye Khiladi? (Who are these new players?)

Ab aate hain sabse interesting part par. Yeh naye "khiladi" hain kaun? Yahan kuch front-runners ke baare mein jaante hain:

#### 1. Skyroot Aerospace
Hyderabad-based yeh startup headline mein tab aaya jab isne November 2022 mein India ka pehla privately built rocket, **Vikram-S**, successfully launch kiya. Bhale hi yeh ek sub-orbital flight (space ke edge tak ja kar wapas aana) thi, isne saabit kar diya ki India ki private industry mein dum hai.
- **Focus:** Small satellite launch vehicles (Vikram series).
- **Technology:** Yeh 3D-printed cryogenic engines par kaam kar rahe hain, jo rockets ko zyada efficient aur kam kharchila banate hain.
- **Goal:** Chhote satellites ko space mein bhejne ka process itna sasta aur aasan bana dena jaise cab book karna.

#### 2. Agnikul Cosmos
Chennai-based Agnikul bhi rocket science mein kamaal kar raha hai. Inka sabse bada innovation hai inka rocket engine, **Agnilet**. Yeh duniya ka pehla single-piece 3D-printed engine hai.

*Analogy:* Sochiye ek machine jisme 1000 alag-alag parts hain aur ek machine jo ek hi solid piece se bani hai. Dusri wali obviously zyada reliable aur sasti hogi. Agnikul yahi kar raha hai.
- **Focus:** Inka launch vehicle hai 'Agnibaan', jo "plug-and-play" engine configuration ke saath aayega. Matlab, payload (satellite ka weight) ke hisab se engines kam ya zyada kiye ja sakte hain.
- **Unique Point:** Yeh India ka pehla private launchpad Sriharikota mein bana chuke hain.

#### 3. Pixxel
Bangalore-based Pixxel rocket nahi, balki satellites banata hai. Lekin yeh koi aam satellites nahi hain. Yeh **hyperspectral imaging satellites** banate hain.

*Analogy:* Ek normal camera (jaise aapke phone mein hai) 3 colours dekhta hai: Red, Green, Blue (RGB). Lekin Pixxel ka satellite aisi "aankhein" hain jo hundreds of colours (spectral bands) dekh sakti hain, jo humari aankhon se invisible hain.
- **Fayda kya hai?** Is technology se:
    - Kheti (Agriculture): Zameen ke andar paani ki kami ka pata lagaya ja sakta hai, ya fasal mein kaun si beemari lag rahi hai, yeh shuru hone se pehle hi pata chal jaayega.
    - Environment: Jungle ki aag, oil spills, ya pollution ko monitor karna bahut aasan ho jaata hai.
    - Mining: Zameen ke neeche kaun se minerals chhupe hain, iska andaza lagaya ja sakta hai.

Yeh toh bas shuruaat hai. Inke alawa bhi Dhruva Space (satellite components), Bellatrix Aerospace (rocket propulsion) jaisi darjanon companies kaam kar rahi hain.

## ISRO ka Role Ab Kya Hoga?

Toh kya ab ISRO ka kaam khatam ho jayega? Bilkul nahi! ISRO ka role ab aur bhi bada aur important ho gaya hai, lekin thoda alag tareeke se.

ISRO ab ek "big brother" ya senior mentor ki tarah kaam karega.
- **Routine Work to Private Sector:** Chhote satellites ko Low Earth Orbit (LEO) tak pahunchane jaise routine kaam ab private companies karengi. Isse ISRO ka bojh kam hoga.
- **Focus on Big Missions:** ISRO ab apne resources ko bade, challenging missions par focus kar payega. Jaise:
    - **Gaganyaan:** Insano ko space mein bhejne ka mission.
    - **Aditya-L1:** Sun ko study karne ka mission.
    - **Deep Space Exploration:** Mars, Venus aur aage ke planets par mission bhejna.
    - **Cutting-edge R&D:** Aisi nayi technology develop karna jo abhi koi nahi kar raha.

Matlab, ISRO ab driver seat se hatkar navigation aur strategy-making par zyada dhyan dega, jabki private companies day-to-day driving karengi.

## Is Private Space Race ke Fayde Kya Hain?

Yeh poora badlav India ke liye ek sunhera mauka hai. Iske kai fayde hain:

- **Innovation aur Competition:** Jab kai companies ek hi cheez ke liye compete karti hain, toh technology behtar hoti hai aur daam kam hote hain. Jaise telecom sector mein Jio ke aane se hua tha.
- **Economic Growth:** Global space economy $500 billion se bhi zyada ki hai. Abhi isme India ka share sirf 2% hai. Private players ke aane se yeh share tezi se badhega, jisse high-tech jobs paida hongi.
- **Faster Access to Space:** Ab startups aur universities ko apne satellite launch karne ke liye saalon tak ISRO ki waiting list mein nahi rehna padega. Woh Skyroot ya Agnikul se jaldi launch karwa sakenge.
- **Global Launch Hub:** India apni low-cost advantage ke kaaran dusre deshon ke satellites launch karne ka ek global hub ban sakta hai, theek waise hi jaise hum IT services mein hain.

## Challenges aur Aage ka Rasta

Har bade badlav mein challenges toh aate hi hain.
- **Funding:** Space missions bahut mehnge hote hain. In startups ko long-term investment ki zaroorat hogi.
- **Regulations:** IN-SPACe ek achhi shuruaat hai, lekin rules aur policies ko clear aur fast banana hoga.
- **Talent Pool:** Hamein aur zyada space engineers aur scientists ki zaroorat padegi.
- **Failure is an Option:** Space ek risky business hai. Rockets fail hote hain. Hamein ek society ke taur par in failures ko accept karke aage badhna seekhna hoga. Yaad rakhiye, SpaceX ke shuruaati rockets bhi fail hue the.

In challenges ke bawajood, future bright dikh raha hai. ISRO ki decades ki legacy aur naye startups ka josh milkar ek unbeatable combination bana rahe hain. India ab sirf space mein ek player nahi, balki ek potential space superpower banne ki raah par hai.
